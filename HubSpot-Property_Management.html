Workflow Architecture

Core automations for leasing, tenant support, and portfolio operations.

The workflow structure below is designed to give the business speed, consistency, and accountability across the full resident and owner journey.

Primary Workflow System

Inquiry Intake & Source ManagementCapture leads from forms, listing portals, referrals, and advertising channels, then standardize source data, property interest, timeline, and lead type.
Qualification & AssignmentRoute inquiries based on criteria such as property type, budget, move-in date, geography, and service line to the correct person or team.
Viewing & Application AutomationTrigger follow-up sequences, appointment reminders, and application progression with clear stage movement and task creation.
Resident OnboardingMove approved applicants into onboarding with document reminders, payment instructions, welcome communication, and move-in readiness tracking.
Maintenance Request ManagementConvert service submissions into prioritized tickets with category logic, SLA targets, vendor routing, internal escalation, and status automation.
Renewal & Retention AutomationTrigger outreach at defined lease milestones, surface at-risk residents, and create internal actions to protect occupancy and retention.

Implementation Scope

How the HubSpot environment would be structured and delivered.

The implementation focuses on structure first, then automation, then reporting, so the system scales cleanly as usage grows.

CRM Structure

  • Custom fields for property type, unit status, lease dates, renewal windows, issue priority, and lead source
  • Relationships across contacts, owners, residents, properties, units, vendors, and service tickets
  • Clear lifecycle stages and pipeline definitions for leasing and support

Workflow Library

  • Lead intake, assignment, nurture, tour scheduling, application tracking, and onboarding
  • Maintenance routing, reminder logic, SLA alerts, and vendor handoffs
  • Renewal sequences, retention alerts, and manager escalation flows

Communication Layer

  • Resident and owner email templates, reminders, confirmations, and status notices
  • Internal alerts, task creation, and team queue management
  • Dashboard views for managers, leasing teams, and operational leadership
Next Steps

Recommended starting point for the build.

Begin with discovery on the current lead sources, leasing stages, maintenance intake process, renewal timing, and owner communication requirements. From there, the HubSpot structure, workflow logic, reporting model, and rollout priorities can be finalized and implemented in a controlled sequence.
